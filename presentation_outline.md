## Presentation Tips

### Timing Guidance:
- Slide 1 (Title): 30 seconds
- Slide 2 (Problem): 1.5 minutes
- Slide 3 (Solution): 2 minutes
- Slide 4 (Results): 2 minutes
- Slide 5 (ROI): 2 minutes
- Slide 6 (Market): 1.5 minutes
- Slide 7 (Roadmap): 2 minutes
- Slide 8 (Risks): 1.5 minutes
- Slide 9 (Metrics): 1 minute
- Slide 10 (Conclusion): 1 minute
- **Total: 15 minutes + 5 minutes Q&A**

### Delivery Best Practices:
- Start with the problem (creates urgency)
- Show technical competence (build credibility)
- Emphasize business value (show ROI thinking)
- Be specific with metrics (demonstrates rigor)
- End with clear next steps (action-oriented)

### Body Language:
- Stand confidently, use hand gestures
- Make eye contact with all interviewers
- Smile when discussing results
- Show enthusiasm about the opportunity

### Handling Questions:
- Listen completely before answering
- Pause to think if needed
- Acknowledge what you don't know
- Offer to dive deeper into backup slides
- Connect answers back to business value
